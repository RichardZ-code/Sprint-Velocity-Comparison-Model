Research Notes

Bolt model

This version uses fixed checkpoints at 10m, 50m, 60m, 100m, and 200m, along with the fixed 80-200 segment sequence. The missing early splits are filled in to keep the acceleration and bend-to-straight transition believable.

Tebogo model

This starts from the fixed Paris 2024 19.46 profile. The 19.18 version is a hypothetical all-out run, with most of the gain moved away from the first 10m and into the straight entry and the finish.
