Research Notes

Bolt model

Built around the fixed 10m, 50m, 60m, 100m, and 200m checkpoints plus the fixed 80-200 segment sequence, then shaped through the remaining early gaps with elite 200m acceleration and curve-running constraints.

Tebogo model

Baseline is the fixed Paris 2024 19.46 profile. The 19.18 line is an all-out hypothesis that trims 0.11 through 100m and 0.17 from 100-200, with the extra gain shifted away from the opening 10m and into straight-entry re-acceleration and late-race speed maintenance.
